Detecting Arbitrage Cycles

In the past several questions, we assumed there were no arbitrage cycles, which are a series of trades that would allow us to start with one unit of currency $i$ and result with more than one unit of currency $i$, making infinite profit possible.

Let's say we are running our algorithm without this assumption. What could we do to determine if there were arbitrage cycles in the graph we construct? Check for any negative cycles. Check if the sum of all shortest paths is negative. It's impossible to detect arbitrage cycles. {{{feedback.q1}}}
