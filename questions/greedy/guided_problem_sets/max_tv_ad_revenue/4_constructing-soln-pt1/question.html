In the previous approaches, we only considered one part of the given information about our advertisers. Instead of greedily choosing advertisers based solely on amount or time, let’s consider the sum ratio product difference between both amount and time and construct the following strategy:

“Sort the advertisers in decreasing order with respect to the value P[i]+L[i] P[i]/L[i] L[i]/P[i] P[i]L[i] L[i]-P[i] P[i]-L[i] , and distribute the time slot in this order.”
