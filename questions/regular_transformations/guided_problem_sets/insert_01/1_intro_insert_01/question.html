For regular language transformation problems, it's important that you fully understand the transformation in question. A good first step is to think about a very simple language and consider what strings are accepted by the transformed language. Consider the following language transformation: $$\mathsf{INSERT01}(L) := \{ x01y \mid xy \in L \}$$
Suppose that $L = \{ 10 \}$. What strings are contained in $\mathsf{INSERT01}(L)$? 0110 1001 1010 0101 0011 1100
