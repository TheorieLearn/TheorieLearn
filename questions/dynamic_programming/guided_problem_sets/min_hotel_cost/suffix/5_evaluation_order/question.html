English Definition
Let $MinCost(i)$ denote the minimum possible cost of traveling from mile $i$ to mile $n$, including the cost of the hotel at mile $i$.

Recursive Definition
$$ \begin{equation} MinCost(i)=\begin{cases} 0 & \text{if $i=n$}\\ HotelCosts[i] & \text{if $i=n-1$}\\ \min \begin{cases} HotelCosts[i] + MinCost(i + 1)\\ HotelCosts[i] + MinCost(i + 2) \end{cases} & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \end{equation} $$ We now need to determine the evaluation order for our memoization structure. In what order do we have to evaluate each subproblem in order to make sure that all of its dependencies are satisfied?

We should fill in a(n) increasing decreasing order with respect to $i$.
