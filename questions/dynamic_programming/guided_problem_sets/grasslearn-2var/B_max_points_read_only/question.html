True Just in case you are curious, below are the complete recursive definitions for $MinMinutes$ and $MaxPoints$. Notice they are quite similar in structure.

Min Minutes
$$ MinMinutes(i,j):= \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if } i \le 0\\ \infty &\text{if } j \ge n \text{ (and }i>0\text{)}\\ \text{min}\begin{cases} MinMinutes(i, j+1), \\ TimeNeeded[j] + MinMinutes(i-Points[i], j+1) \end{cases} & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} $$
Max Points
$$ MaxPoints(i,j):= \begin{cases} -\infty & \text{if } i < 0\\ 0 &\text{if } j \ge n \text{ or }i=0\\ \text{max}\begin{cases} MaxPoints(i, j+1), \\ Points[j] + MaxPoints(i-TimeNeeded[i], j+1) \end{cases} & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} $$
