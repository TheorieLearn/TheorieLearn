We will define two subproblems. Let $A = (L_1L_2)^*$, and let $B = (L_1L_2)^*L_1$.

Let $isInA(i) = True$ if and only if $s[1..i] \in A$. Similarly, let $isInB(i) = True$ if and only if $s[1..i] \in B$.

Suppose that we have a nonempty prefix of $s[1..i]$.

We've established that if $s[1..i] \in A$, then it must have a suffix in $L_2$. The corresponding prefix must be in B A B* A* .
Now, suppose that $s[1..i] \in B$. By the definition of $B$, it must have a suffix in L_1 L_2 L_1L_1 L_2L_2 . The corresponding prefix must be in A B AB B* .
