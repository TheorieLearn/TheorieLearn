This is not the only approach, and we are not trying to suggest that it is the best approach. --- It's often helpful to view regular expressions from the perspective of blocks (introduced in the previous problem). In particular, "not containg substring" conditions can often be rephrased as a constraint on the blocks in the string. What are the constraints on the blocks in strings not containing 000?
