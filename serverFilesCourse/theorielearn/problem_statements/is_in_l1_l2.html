Given a string $s[1..n]$, determine if $s \in (L_1L_2)^*$

For ease of notation, let $A = (L_1L_2)^*$.

Assume that you are given two oracles, $isInL_1()$ and $isInL_2()$, that can determine in constant time whether a string is in $L_1$ or $L_2$, respectively.

You can also assume that $\varepsilon \notin L_1$ and $\varepsilon \notin L_2$.
