You've just been appointed as the new organzier of a party at Giggle. Giggle employees are organized into a strict hierarchy, a tree (where there is a directed edge from a supervisor to their subordinate) with the company president at the root. The all-knowing oracles in Human Resources have assigned a real number to each employee measuring how "fun" the employee is.


There are two restrictions on the guest list:
  • An employee cannot attend the party if their immediate supervisor is present
  • The president of the company must attend the party (it's her company after all)
Assume each of the employees are given a label of $1,\ldots,n$ and you have access to array $Fun$ where $Fun[i]$ is the fun that employee $i$ brings to the party

Give an algorithm that makes a guest list for the party that maximizes the sum of the "fun" ratings of the guests.
